Ethan Hunt

x

Tom Cruise as Ethan Hunt

Tom Cruise reprises his role as Ethan Hunt, the IMF (Impossible Mission Force) agent known for his high-stakes missions and death-defying stunts. In this movie, Ethan is tasked with recovering stolen plutonium, which has fallen into the hands of a terrorist group called the Apostles. They plan to use it to create nuclear weapons, threatening millions of lives.

Ethan Hunt demonstrates a range of exceptional skills that make him a top IMF agent. He’s a master of combat, both hand-to-hand and with firearms, and has advanced driving and piloting abilities, which help him navigate high-stakes chases. His expertise in infiltration, disguise, and stealth allow him to slip into dangerous situations undetected, while his quick thinking and intelligence enable him to adapt instantly to unexpected challenges. Above all, Ethan’s unwavering determination, loyalty to his team, and commitment to protecting lives drive him to take on extreme risks, often pushing beyond normal limits to accomplish his missions.

Julia Meade

x

Michelle Monaghan as Julia Meade

Michelle Monaghan returns as Julia Meade, Ethan Hunt’s estranged wife. Her role is smaller but emotionally significant, as she represents Ethan’s past and the sacrifices he’s made for his life as an IMF agent.

Julia Meade, Ethan Hunt’s estranged wife, demonstrates skills that make her a resilient, resourceful character. As a trained nurse, she provides essential medical care in challenging environments, showing composure and focus even in high-stress situations. Her adaptability helps her assist the IMF team, working with Luther to keep people safe during a nuclear threat. Julia’s empathy and emotional strength allow her to offer Ethan closure, enabling him to move forward without guilt. Though not an agent, her expertise and calm under pressure make her a strong, supportive presence.

Benji Dunn

x

Simon Pegg as Benji Dunn

Simon Pegg returns as Benji Dunn, the tech specialist and field agent for the IMF team. Benji has been a fan favorite since his first appearance in Mission: Impossible III.

Benji Dunn, played by Simon Pegg, is a skilled tech expert and increasingly capable field agent. His technical abilities in hacking, surveillance, and gadget management are essential to the team’s success, and he’s adept at using disguises for covert infiltration. While he’s not primarily a combatant, Benji shows bravery in the face of danger and can hold his own when needed, even facing villains like Solomon Lane. Known for his humor, Benji lightens tense moments and boosts team morale, making him a loyal, resourceful, and invaluable member of the IMF team.

Lisa Fraust

x

Rebecca Ferguson as Lisa Fraust

Rebecca Ferguson returns as Ilsa Faust, the former MI6 agent who has a complicated relationship with Ethan Hunt (Tom Cruise). Ilsa is a skilled operative with her own agenda, and her storyline adds both tension and intrigue to the film.

Ilsa Faust, played by Rebecca Ferguson, is a highly skilled former MI6 agent with exceptional combat abilities, precise marksmanship, and expertise in stealth and infiltration. Her quick thinking and adaptability make her a valuable asset in unpredictable situations, while her skill in high-speed motorcycle and vehicle handling aids in intense chases. Ilsa’s independent and tactical mindset, paired with her strong sense of morality, adds complexity to her role, making her both a powerful ally and, at times, a wildcard in her interactions with Ethan Hunt and the IMF team.

Luther stickell

x

Ving Rhames as Luther Stickell

Ving Rhames reprises his role as Luther Stickell, the longtime IMF agent and trusted friend of Ethan Hunt. As the team's hacker and tech expert, Luther is known for his loyalty and skill, and in Fallout, he’s more essential than ever.

Luther Stickell, played by Ving Rhames, is the IMF team’s veteran hacker and technical expert. His skills in hacking, surveillance, and communications are vital for navigating complex missions and ensuring team coordination. Known for his quick thinking and problem-solving abilities, Luther is also an emotional anchor for the team, providing loyalty and stability, especially to his close friend Ethan Hunt. Although typically behind the scenes, Luther is capable in the field, offering both tactical support and a calming presence that helps keep the team grounded in high-stress situations

August Walker

x

Henry Cavill as August Walker

August Walker, is a significant addition to Mission: Impossible – Fallout, where he plays a complex and physically imposing antagonist to Tom Cruise’s Ethan Hunt.

August Walker, portrayed by Henry Cavill, is a skilled CIA assassin known for his brutal combat abilities, exceptional marksmanship, and physical strength. He excels in close-quarters fighting, using direct, powerful techniques, and is highly proficient with firearms. Trained in stealth and covert operations, Walker can infiltrate high-risk situations undetected, completing missions with a ruthless, goal-oriented focus. His cold, calculated approach contrasts sharply with Ethan Hunt’s moral compass, making Walker a relentless and dangerous adversary who prioritizes mission objectives over human life.
